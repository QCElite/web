QC Elite is a community of mentors, students, parents, and alumni working side by side to promote science and engineering. Inspiring young engineers for seventeen years, we strive to instill problem-solving skills, teamwork, and technical knowledge through friendly coopertition. Students work alongside skilled professionals to develop a respect, understanding, and interest in STEM.
We are a team in the FIRST Robotics Competition, serving both the Iowa and Illinois Quad Cities.

QC Elite would like to give a big thank you to all our generous sponsors!
